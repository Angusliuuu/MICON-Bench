[Verification Checkpoints]

A. Instruction Following
- A_check_1 (H1): Does the image include all specified objects?
- A_check_2: Are the objects arranged as instructed?
- A_check_3: Are there no extra or missing elements?

B. Identity / Fidelity
- B_check_1 (H2): Does each object's identity strictly match its reference?
- B_check_2: Are key attributes (color, texture, shape) preserved?
- B_check_3: Are the object details accurate and recognizable?

C. Structure / Geometry
- C_check_1: Are the spatial relationships between objects natural?
- C_check_2: Are the proportions and sizes realistic?
- C_check_3: Is the perspective and depth correct?

D. Cross-Reference Consistency
- D_check_1: Are objects from different references integrated without conflict?
- D_check_2: Are style and lighting consistent across objects?
- D_check_3: Are there no contradictions or jarring transitions?
- D_check_4: Does the background match the instruction/reference?

G. Overall Usability
- G_check_1: Does the scene appear natural and cohesive?
- G_check_2: Are the lighting, shadows, and overall aesthetics consistent?
